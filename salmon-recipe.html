Garlic Salmon with Lemon Butter

Less than 10 mins to prep, easy dish and very tasty

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Line a baking tray with tin foil, and pre heat the oven to 200deg fan. Wash the rice and put in rice cooker. In a pan, on medium heat, add the butter, minced garlic, and lemon juice and mix for 3 minutes then take off the heat.

step 2

Take your salmon fillets and washed Asparagus tips and season thoroughly on all sides with salt and pepper and place on baking tray.

step 3

Pour the lemon butter sauce over the salmon and asparagus and put tray in the oven for 15 minutes.

step 3

While it is cooking, cook half of your lemon into slices and finely chop the parsley. Once the salmon and rice has cooked plate up, garnish with lemon slices, lemon juice and parlsey and enjoy :)

ingredients

4 Salmon Fillets

4 tbsp lemon juice

100g of salted butter

4 cloves garlic, minced

12 asparagus tips

200g white basmati rice

fresh parsley

1 lemon, for garnishing

tools

Pan

Sharp Knife

Baking Tray and tin foil

Rice cooker is optimal

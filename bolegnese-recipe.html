Pork Bolognese

An easy bolognese dish that compliments a glass of wine. Use any type of pasta or cheese.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Add olive oil to the pan on medium heat and add Onion to the pan (diced). After 2mins, add garlic (minced) and mix for 1min. Add your Pork Mince and break apart, seasoning with salt and pepper.

step 2

After Pork has become brown on all sides, add pasta to a pot with boiling water and leave it on high heat for 10mins, occasionally stirring.

step 3

At the same time, add the chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, beef stock and mixed herbs. Let it simmer for 10 mins, add a splash of water if too dry. Stir occasionally, tasting throughout adding more salt/herbs as you go for your own preference.

step 3

After the pasta has cooked turn off the heat and drain the pasta, turn off the heat to the pasta and add grated cheese. Stir the cheese into the sauce and serve up, garnishing with parsley and more cheese to your own preference.

ingredients

500g Pork Mince

1 White Onion

Can of chopped Tomatoes

Beef Stock

3 cloves of garlic

1 tbsp Tomato Puree

300g pasta

mixed herbs

Grated Cheese

chopped parsley for garnish

tools

Pot and Pan

Sharp Knife

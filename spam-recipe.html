Spam Fried Rcie

Easy for bulk meals, a traditional spam fried rice.

prep time

<5 min.

cook time

25 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Firstly, take the span out and cut into small 1x1cm cubes. Add oil to a pan and on high heat fry the spam cubes until crispy on all sides. While this is happening, add your rice to the rice cooker and start.

step 2

after Spam is ready, transfer to a clean bowl. Using another bowl, add 300ml of water along with the peas and micrwave for 3 minutes. Make sure the bowl is microwavable and is covered by a pierced sheet of cling film.

step 3

When the rice has finished, add to original pan on medium along with your spam and cooked peas, add soy sauce and mix. Next crack 4 eggs over the pan and mix for 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, add more soy sauce depending on your preference.

step 3

After the egg has cooked through, turn off the heat and plate up and enjoy your easy to make, Spam Fried Rice.

ingredients

1 tin of spam (200g)

200g of rice

200g of peas

4 eggs

3 tbsp soy sauce

tools

Pan

Sharp Knife

Rice cooker is optimal

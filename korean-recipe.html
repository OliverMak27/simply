Korean Fried Spicy Chicken

Tasty Spicy Korean Fried Chicken and Rice is a favourite, very easy to make. When re-heated, tastes even better.

prep time

<5 min.

cook time

25 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Firstly, start cooking rice in rice cooker. Cut chicken into smaller pieces and transfer to a bowl. Season with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Mix thoroughly. In a seperate bowl add potato starch, you can add more if you need to when you need it. Coat each piece of chicken and set aside.

step 2

Heat up some oil and fry the chicken for 3-5mins on each side until chicken is cooked through. It is optimal to re fry chicken for 2 minutes each side. Once done, transfer chicken to a cooling rack.

step 3

In a pan, add soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, honey, minced garlic, grated ginger, sesame oil and gochujang. Heat on high and mix until it starts to bubble, add the chicken and mix until evenly coated.

step 3

Sprinkle sesame seeds and chopped spring onion and plate up with rice and enjoy :)

ingredients

1kg breast or boneless thigh chicken

200g of rice

vegetable oil for frying

100g potato starch

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp sesame oil

3 tbsp mirin

2 tbsp brown sugar

1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

2 tbsp honey

2 garlic cloves minced

1 tbsp grated ginger

1 tbsp gochujang

Sesame seeds and spring onion to garnish

tools

Pan

Sharp Knife

Rice cooker is optimal
